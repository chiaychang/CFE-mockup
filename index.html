Marketing Research

We know your pain points when marketing to Engineers.

Marketers in charge of targeting technical audiences are grappling with the shift toward online and content marketing and how to implement an approach that effectively engages an engineering audience.

They need to understand the types of content engineers require to better perform in their jobs, and where, why and how they look for and use this content in the evaluation and purchase process (or, “throughout the buying cycle”).

While research exists in the consumer and broad B2B markets about content marketing and published surveys touch on the technical professional’s use of content, we identified a gap and embarked upon taking a deeper dive into this area to educate and inform marketers of the most effective ways to target this highly technical audience.
